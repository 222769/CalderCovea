Services we offer

Life

The policies and insurance that we provide in the category for life will cover our customers from any accident for life, ranging from accidental death cover to accident protection. Our customers can rely on us to ensure that if anything happens we will have their back, it is our customer guarantee.


Motor

Under the motor category, we have a wide variety of policies that will meet various different needs for our customers. There are insurances for vehicles used in a business, to commercial good vehicles and small motors used in everyday life. Most of our policies in any category are flexible enough that they can be tailored to each and every one of our customers.


Business

Polices under our business category will be suitable for any business owner, these could range from policies for small up and coming businesses, to already established large companies. The policy will be flexible to support a business in any marketplace.


Household

For households, we provide a flexible insurance and policy plan that will be tailored exactly for the customer’s requirements. The policies will work for small houses and property, large houses and even apartments in a building complex.
